What Is Passage

Your documents, finally understood.

The immigration process means navigating dense legal forms, unfamiliar agencies, and critical deadlines — often in a language that isn't your first. Passage breaks that barrier.

Paste or drop any immigration document and instantly get a side-by-side translation, a plain-English summary at your reading level, automatic privacy redaction, and a checklist of documents typically required for your process.

Passage is a translation and comprehension aid. It does not give legal advice.

Built-In Features

Four tools. One mission.

01
Side-by-Side Translation

Your document in English and your chosen language, displayed together for easy comparison. Both sides include read-aloud voice playback so you can listen, not just read.

Voice included
02
Privacy Shield

Automatically redacts sensitive personal information — names, dates, ID numbers, case numbers, and addresses — before you share a screenshot. Toggle each category on or off. Hover any block to peek.

Local only · No data leaves
03
Simple English Summary

Legal language re-explained at three reading levels: Basic (everyday words for early English learners), Intermediate (clear language with brief definitions), and Full Detail (thorough breakdown with headers).

Three reading levels
04
Documents Needed

Passage identifies the immigration process your document belongs to and generates a general list of documents that are typically associated with it — so you know what to prepare. Informational only, not legal advice.

Informational only
